## 5. DM to Nutrition Influencers on Instagram (20 DMs)

> Strategy: Personalize the first line for each influencer — reference a specific post or piece of content they've shared. This is the single most important thing you can do to get a response. Generic DMs get ignored.
>
> Best time to send: 2:00 PM PST on launch day (when your listing already has momentum — it gives you social proof to reference).

---

**Template:**

Hi [Name] 👋

I loved your recent post on [specific topic they posted about — e.g., "hidden carbs in keto sauces" / "meal planning during pregnancy" / "Mediterranean diet meal prep"]. Really resonated.

I'm the founder of NutriAI — an AI nutritionist that works entirely through WhatsApp (no app download). [Specific hook relevant to their niche — see variations below].

We launched on Product Hunt today and we're getting great traction. I'd love to offer you free access to try it, and if you find it genuinely useful for your audience, I'd be grateful for any mention — no pressure at all.

Would you be open to trying it?

https://nutri-ai.app

[Your name]

---

**Niche-specific hook variations (swap into the template above):**

**For keto influencers:**
> NutriAI has a strict keto enforcement mode that detects hidden carbs in sauces, dressings, and processed foods — something I haven't seen any consumer app do properly. I think your audience would find it genuinely useful.

**For pregnancy/maternal health influencers:**
> NutriAI has a pregnancy mode that tracks folate, iron, and calcium daily — and flags foods that aren't safe during pregnancy. I built it because my wife was pregnant and I couldn't find anything like it. I think your audience of expecting moms might really appreciate it.

**For Mediterranean diet influencers:**
> NutriAI has a Mediterranean diet mode with macro targets and coaching tuned to the diet's principles — olive oil, legumes, fish. The AI gives feedback in the context of the diet, not just generic calorie counting.

**For general nutrition/wellness influencers:**
> The core idea is removing friction — most people don't stick to nutrition apps because they require too much effort. NutriAI lives in WhatsApp, which people already use constantly. Your audience might appreciate something that meets them where they already are.

**For Israeli / Hebrew-speaking influencers:**
> NutriAI is fully bilingual — Hebrew and English. You can log "אכלתי חומוס עם פיתה ושניים עגבניות" and it understands and tracks it perfectly. I think that's pretty rare for a nutrition app.

---

> **Note on volume and timing:** Send DMs to 5 influencers right at launch (midnight cohort), 5 more at 9 AM, 5 more at 2 PM, and the final 5 at 6 PM. This keeps you responsive as replies come in, rather than managing 20 conversations simultaneously.
