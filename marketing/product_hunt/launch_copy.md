## Long Description (500–800 words)

### Meet NutriAI — The Nutritionist That's Already On Your Phone

Most nutrition apps fail for the same reason: they require you to install something, learn a new interface, and build a new habit from scratch. Life gets busy. The app collects dust. You give up.

**NutriAI works differently. It lives in WhatsApp — the app you already open 50 times a day.**

---

### The Problem

Getting quality nutrition guidance is hard. Registered dietitians are expensive and inaccessible for most people. Generic calorie apps are tedious — logging a meal requires clicking through hundreds of food entries, and they never account for *your* specific goals. Worse, they have zero personality. Nobody sticks with something that feels like a tax form.

For people with specific dietary needs — those following strict keto, pregnant women monitoring their intake, or people navigating the Mediterranean diet — the gap is even wider. Off-the-shelf apps offer vague suggestions and no real enforcement.

---

### The Solution

NutriAI is a full-featured AI nutritionist that operates entirely through WhatsApp (and optionally through a web UI). You talk to it the way you'd talk to a real dietitian — in plain language, in your own language.

**Here's what it does:**

- **Photo logging** — Snap a photo of your meal and NutriAI identifies it, estimates calories and macros, and logs it instantly. No manual entry.
- **Natural language logging** — "I had a bowl of oatmeal with almond milk and blueberries" is enough. NutriAI handles the rest.
- **Multi-diet modes** — Switch between Keto, Mediterranean, Intermittent Fasting, and Pregnancy mode. Each mode changes how NutriAI coaches you, what it warns you about, and how it calculates your goals.
- **Strict keto enforcement** — NutriAI flags hidden carbs, warns you before you break ketosis, and tracks your net carbs in real time. No other consumer app does this level of keto enforcement.
- **Pregnancy mode** — Tracks folate, iron, calcium, and avoids foods that are unsafe during pregnancy. A genuinely rare feature in the consumer nutrition space.
- **Hebrew + English** — Fully bilingual. NutriAI detects your language and responds in kind. Israeli users can log "אכלתי סלט עם טחינה וחצי פיתה" and get an accurate breakdown.
- **Daily summaries and trend insights** — Ask "how did I do this week?" and get a real answer.
- **Free tier available** — Basic tracking is free. No credit card required to start.

---

### The Tech

NutriAI is powered by Claude AI for natural language understanding and food analysis, Flask as the backend, and Twilio for the WhatsApp integration. The result is a system that feels conversational, not robotic — because it's built on one of the most capable language models available.

---

### Why Now

WhatsApp has 2 billion+ active users. Messaging-first interfaces are having a moment. People are increasingly comfortable interacting with AI in conversational settings. The infrastructure finally exists to make this work seamlessly — and NutriAI is one of the first nutrition products to fully embrace that.

The nutrition coaching market is massive and underserved by software. Personal dietitians cost $100–$300/month. NutriAI delivers a meaningful fraction of that value for a fraction of the cost — to anyone with a phone number.
